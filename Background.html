Background

My name is Katie Martin. I am a student at Auburn University pursuing an undergraduate degree in Chemical Engineering with a double minor in Information Technology and Business. I am an Alexander City native from Alabama who spent her childhood growing up playing every little league sport and constructing fictional villages with my older brother. At a young age, I learned the importance of a strong work ethic and saw it modeled by both my parents and grandparents in their everyday lives. I learned that success means nothing if you have no one to share it with and that to become a leader one must first be willing to serve. I am a firm believer in that one's circumstance does not dictate one's future or his or her ability to be happy. Life is what you make it. I am thankful for my past and upbringing because it has molded me into the strong and fearless woman I am today. I enjoy being outdoors and working with my hands, but most importantly, I love people.
